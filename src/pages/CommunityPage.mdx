## How to Get Involved

Getting started in our community is easy! Here are the main ways you can participate:

1. **Join our Discord** - Connect with other members in real-time
2. **Contribute to Projects** - Help build the next generation of security tools
3. **Share Knowledge** - Write blog posts, create tutorials, or give talks
4. **Participate in Research** - Collaborate on academic papers and security research
5. **Attend Events** - Join our virtual meetups, workshops, and conferences

### Community Guidelines

We maintain a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members:

- **Be Respectful** - Treat all community members with respect and professionalism
- **Share Knowledge** - Help others learn and grow in their cybersecurity journey
- **Collaborate Openly** - Work together on projects and share your expertise
- **Follow Ethics** - All activities must be legal and ethical
- **Give Credit** - Acknowledge the work and contributions of others
